Indian Society for Technical Education

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national-level organization committed to enhancing the quality of technical education in India. With a vast network of faculty and student members across the country, ISTE plays a key role in promoting research, skill development, and industry-academia interaction. The ISTE Student Chapter at GECK is an active hub for innovation, organizing technical workshops, competitions, seminars, and hands-on training programs. It serves as a bridge between students and the professional world, equipping them with the latest technological skills, leadership qualities, and problem-solving abilities.
